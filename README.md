# 🛒 Retail Sales Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **retail sales data** using SQL to uncover business insights and solve real-world analytical problems.

The dataset includes transaction-level information such as customer demographics, product categories, sales amount, and purchase timing.

The project demonstrates the use of **SQL for data analysis, business intelligence, and decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze retail transaction data and extract meaningful insights to support business decisions.

It aims to:

* Perform data exploration and cleaning
* Apply SQL concepts like **aggregation, grouping, filtering, and window functions**
* Solve real-world business problems
* Identify trends in customer behavior and sales performance

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following columns:

* **transactions_id** – Unique transaction identifier
* **sale_date** – Date of purchase
* **sale_time** – Time of purchase
* **customer_id** – Unique customer ID
* **gender** – Customer gender
* **age** – Customer age
* **category** – Product category
* **quantity** – Number of items purchased
* **price_per_unit** – Price per item
* **cogs** – Cost of goods sold
* **total_sale** – Total transaction value

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* SQL (PostgreSQL / MySQL)
* Data Analysis
* Aggregation & Grouping
* Case Statements
  
---

## 📈 Key Insights

* 🛍️ Sales vary significantly across different product categories
* 👥 Customer demographics (age & gender) influence purchasing behavior
* 📅 Certain months perform better in terms of sales
* 💰 A small group of customers contributes to high revenue
* ⏰ Sales distribution varies across different times of the day
